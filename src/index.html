Seasonal trends

On the graphic, we can observe that multiple products follow a seasonal trend. Preselected products are shown in the combobox. They are sorted by the one with the most visible seasonal trends to those with smaller seasonal trends. With this, we can see that some products depends of the season of each product production. It is the case for carrots which have their lower price in september and their higher price in june. The same applies for onions and oranges (not sure oranges).
